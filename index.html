About me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow under the VISTA programme at the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, York University. I work with Prof. Michael Brown on topics related to Computer Vision and Image Processing. I defended my Ph.D. in September 2017 at Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT-M) where I had been working at the Department of Electrical Engineering with Prof. A. N. Rajagopalan.
